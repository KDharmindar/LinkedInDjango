NEW CONNECTOR CAMPAIGN

Want to grow your network and find new leads? Start new Connector Campaign.

  • Set up steps with personalized automated messages for connection requests or a follow-up messages to be sent a few hours or days after connecting, set another automated follow-up messages if no response is received in a few days. This is done by clicking on the Steps tab inside the Connector Campaign.
  • Search for new leads by keyword, location, industry, company, title. To start a new search, go to the Search page on the top menu, and click on 'Start new search' on the right side.
  • After the search is completed select people you would like to be added to Connector Campaign and click 'Add selected people to Connector Campaign', or add all new people found by clicking 'Add all new people to Connector Campaign'.
